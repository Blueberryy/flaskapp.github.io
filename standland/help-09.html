Stand count does not match between iPhone and Apple Watch.

If your stand count is different between Apple Watch and iPhone, Standland's data source may be set to "Steps". The data source needs to be "Stand Hours".

  1. Open the Standland app
  2. Tap the menu icon in the lower right corner of the screen.
  3. Tap "Settings" on the bottom tab.
  4. Select "Stand Hours" as "Data Source".

If the above does not help, please follow the instructions on this page for more detailed settings.
