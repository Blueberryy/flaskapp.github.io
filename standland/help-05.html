Backup and restore

Standland uses data from the app and Apple's Health app.

You can save these data by backing up with iCloud and iTunes (requires encryption). And you can restore your device from the backup.

If you choose iTunes for backup, you need to turn on the "Encrypt iPhone backup" option.
More information: About encrypted backups in iTunes

We recommend doing an encrypted backup with iTunes. This backup includes certain information that other backups don't.
