Data doesn't show up

If your data doesn't show up in Standland, please check the permission in Apple's Health app.
Standland needs access to the Health app

If you grant the read permission and still there is no data, please open the Health app, and see if there is Steps data (or Stand Hours data if you use Apple Watch).
If the Health app is unstable, third party apps cannot access the data.
In that case, restarting your iPhone might fix the issue.
