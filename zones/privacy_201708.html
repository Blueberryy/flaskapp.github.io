1. Information Collection And Use

1-1. Health Data

We will use the Apple HealthKit APIs to read and/or write certain data to the Health App Database on your device (the “Health Data”).
Such information may include, for example:

  • Workouts
  • Heart Rate
  • Distance
  • Active Energy

We will use your Health Data to show you the workout details and the heart rate zones.

We will not use Health Data for marketing or advertising purposes. Health Data will be accessed locally through Apple HealthKit and will not be stored or cached by the app. This app cannot read from or write to the Health Database without your consent. Health Data will not be sent to our server.

1-2. Location Data

This app will start to collect location data from sources such as GPS, Wi-Fi and cell towers once you install the App and consent to the App’s tracking your location (the “Location Data”).

We will use your Location Data to show you the route map of your workout.

We will not use Location Data for marketing or advertising purposes. Location Data is stored locally and not sent to our server. This app cannot read your location without your consent. You can adjust your mobile device settings by following the instructions here.

1-3. Crash Log

This app uses Crashlytics, a crash reporting system which allows us to become quickly aware of any problems by sending anonymous crash reports. If this app crashes during normal use, information about the crash (such as the time of the crash, the device model, iOS version, and the state of the application at the time of the crash) will be sent to the servers of Crashlytics.
The privacy policy of Crashlytics is available at Crashlytics web site.
