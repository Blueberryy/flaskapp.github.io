Wrong Total Active Calories or Distance

Total active calorie and total distance of workout measured by Zones are based on values registered in Apple Health.
The causes of these incorrect values are as follows

  1. Apple Watch (watchOS) could not calculate the correct value
  2. Zones could not collect the correct samples
Please investigate the cause by the following.

Re-collect sample data

You can recollect from the data registered in Apple Health on the workout editing view.

  1. Open "Zones" app on iPhone.
  2. Tap the workout and show workout detail view.
  3. Tap the pencil icon (edit button) on the top right.
  4. Tap distance or active calorie.
  5. Select Apple Watch source

The app re-collects the sample data from the selected source and show the total value.

If it changes to the correct value

Zones could not collect the correct data or registration could be delayed due to some troubles of Apple Health on Apple Watch.
After restarting Apple Watch and iPhone, try measuring new workout and check whether it is updated properly.

In the case of swimming, the range of the distance sample is as long as 1 minute, so it may not contain data for the last minute of workout. Please note.

If it remains the wrong value

It is possible that Apple Watch (watchOS) could not calculate the correct value.
Check the original data (sample) with Apple Health.

For active calories, please select the source of Apple Watch and "Update" your workout before trying the following.
(By updating, samples of active calorie are linked to workout.)

Show sample data on Apple Health

  1. Open Apple "Health" on iPhone
  2. Tap "Health Data" on tab
  3. Tap "Activity"
  4. Tap "Workouts"
  5. Tap "Show All Data"
  6. Tap the workout
  7. Tap "Total Active Energy" or "Total *** Deistance" of "WORKOUT SAMPLES"

If there are samples with wrong values

Please check settings to accuracy increases of your Apple Watch.

  • Apple Support: Get the most accurate measurements using your Apple Watch
  • Apple Support: Calibrating your Apple Watch Series 1 or earlier for improved Workout and Activity accuracy
The already registered sample data will not be updated even if you change the setting.
Please measure and check the new workout.

If The number of samples is small or none

There was a possibility that there was some trouble with Apple Watch.
If you are going to workout again and still in the same state, please check the following.

  • Is it correctly measured by Apple's "workout" application?
  • Try restarting Apple Watch and iPhone.

Some activities do not record distance samples.
Rowing, Stairs, Step Training, etc.

If samples are recorded in duplicate

Confirm the setting of the source (app) registering the sample.
Also note that if you are synchronizing Health data on iCloud, data registered with other devices will also be synchronized.
