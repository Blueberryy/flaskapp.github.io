Can not display / record workouts

1. Check the permissions of health

  1. Open Apple Health app.
  2. Tap the "source" of the tab bar
  3. Tap Zones
  4. Turn on all write / read switches.

2. Check your Workouts

You can check whether there are records in Apple Health app.

  1. Open Apple Health app.
  2. Tap the "Activity"
  3. Tap the "Workouts"
  4. Tap the "Show All Data"

3. Check duration of workout

Workout of less than 30 seconds will not be recorded.
If the exercise time is less than 30 seconds are not recorded.
Please exercising for more than 30 seconds.
