Exercise Intensity and Max Heart Rate

Exercise Intensity is calculated by percentage of maximum heart rate. (%HRmax, %MHR)

You can change the max heart in Settings > HEART RATE ZONES > Max Heart Rate.

The max heart rate is calculated by the following formula.
211 - (0.64 × Age)

It is also possible to edit the max heart rate directly without using the above equation..
When you tap the value of Max Heart Rate, the keyboard is displayed.
