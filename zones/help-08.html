Can not run app simultaneously with other app on Apple Watch

You can not run multiple applications simultaneously for measuring workout on Apple Watch.
Even if you launch several applications in succession, the application that you started later becomes effective.
This will be Apple Watch's specification, so even for non-Zones applications.

A green human icon will be displayed at the top of the clock screen while the workout is running.
