Heart rate is not displayed on Apple Watch

If "Measuring ..." continues for a long time, heart rate can not be acquired.
In this situation, please confirm the following.

Confirm Apple Health permission

Please make sure that the permission of reading heart rate in Apple Health app.
Please see Can not display / record workout for access permission.

Confirm setting of "Workout Power Saving Mode"

There is a setting of Workout Power Saving Mode in Apple Watch app > My Watch > General.
If the Workout Power Saving Mode is ON, heart rate will not be measured. Please turn it off and try.

However, turning off the power saving mode increases the consumption of Apple Watch's battery.
It is desirable to turn off if you are concerned about battery consumption when doing a long workout.
In that case, the Zones app will either be in the "Measuring ..." state, or it will be updated once every few minutes.
Please give up on the display of heart rate, scroll down and use the screen which displayed distance and calorie consumption.

For the power saving mode, refer to the following Apple support page.
Work out with your Apple Watch - Save power while you work out

When using external heart rate monitor

Zones reads your heart rate registered in Apple Watch's health (HealthKit).
Therefore, the external heart rate monitor needs to be connected to Apple Watch via Bluetooth.
Please check How to measure the heart rate.
