PicTack is a fun, quick-and-easy drawing game!
Play alone or do it with friends. The app gives you a title. You work on your best drawing within the given time.
If playing with friends, compare and score the drawings. Laugh over outcomes.

FEATURES

DRAW WITH FRIENDS
Up to 5 people can play PicTack together. (Bluetooth or WiFi are required)
Of course, you can enjoy it on your own.
COMPARE DRAWINGS
After the drawing time is up, multiple drawings will appear one-after-another on your iPhone.
Just tap Thumb Up/Down buttons as many times as you like to score the drawings.
IN CASE YOU FORGET
Don't worry if you can't remember what a "Bulldozer" looks like.
PicTack can give you sample pictures.
60 DRAWING SUBJECTS
60 subjects are free-of-charge, and there's more. Enjoy simple ones "For Children", one's that make you laugh "For Parties", and even the ones for maniacs "For IT Engineers" (The additional ones are charged). More entertaining subjects are on their way.
SHARE YOUR MASTERPIECE
You can save and even share your masterpiece. Tweet your drawings. (iOS5+ is required)
PERFECT FOR THESE MOMENTS
- When you have a little spare time
- For parties
- When playing with a child

HOW TO PLAY

How to start a multi-player game.

  1. With iPhone, go to Settings > General > Bluetooth and turn it ON. (Also runs on same Wi-Fi.)
  2. Let the game Host tap the button "Create a Game".
  3. Other playing members need to tap "Join a Game". The waiting screen will appear.
  4. When all members are ready, the Host can tap "START"!

Multi-player games cannot be arranged through internet.
The host alone sees the buttons START and NEXT SUBJECT.
