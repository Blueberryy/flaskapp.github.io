How do I back up?

Please use the backup function of your calendar.

iCloud Calendars

  1. Open "Setting" app on your iPhone
  2. Show iCloud Settings
  3. Tap "iCloud"
  4. Turn on "Calendar"
Check the above settings and back up on iCloud.
