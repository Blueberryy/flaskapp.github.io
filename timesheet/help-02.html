No Data of Several Months Ago

Check the synchronization settings of calendars.

  1. Open "Settings" on your iPhone
  2. Tap "Calendar"
  3. Change "All Events" on Sync
  4. Open the "Calendar" app of iPhone, and wait for data to be synchronized
