FAQ

The data doesn't show up.
Please enable read/write permissions on Health app.
1. Open Health app
2. Tap Sources
3. Select FitPort
4. Turn on items that you want to track. (Update & Share)
Can I use the app on iPad?
No. This app requires HealthKit and runs only with iOS 8 on iPhone and iPod touch.
