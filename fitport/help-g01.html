FitPort needs access to Health

FitPort needs access to Apple Health app (HealthKit)

You can grant or deny permission separately for each type of data.

Write:
Add data to HealthKit.
Read FitPort's data on HealthKit
Read:
Read data (Include data from other apps)

It is recommended to turn on both permissions on FitPort.
If all permissions are granted, FitPort can not delete data from other apps. And your health data will never be sent to our servers by FitPort.

How to change permissions

  1. Open Health app
  2. Tap Sources
  3. Tap FitPort
  4. Turn on items that you want to track. (Write & Read)
