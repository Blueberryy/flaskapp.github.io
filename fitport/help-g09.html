Apple Watch app basics

Install FitPort on your Apple Watch

  1. Open the Apple Watch app on your iPhone and tap the My Watch tab.
  2. Scroll to FitPort, then tap it.
  3. Turn on Show App on Apple Watch.

Features

  • Shows Steps, Distance, and Active Energy
  • You can see the last 7 days of data in a graph by turning the Digital Crown
  • Add Complications (Steps only)

Change Units and Goals

Units and goals are synchronized from iPhone.

If the values are not synchronized, try changing it in FitPort Settings on your iPhone, with FitPort open on your Apple Watch.

If it does not work, there is a possibility that communication between Apple Watch and iPhone is not working properly. Please restart your Apple Watch and try again.
Restart your Apple Watch

Complications

You can see Steps on your watch face.

Depending on the type of Complications, there are some that can display the number of step counts and one that can display a pie chart of step count goal.
