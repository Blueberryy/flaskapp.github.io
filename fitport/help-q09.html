How do I check my BMI?

BMI is calculated from weight and height automatically, and you can see it on the Weight graph view.
However FitPort doesn't write BMI to the Health app automatically.

1. At first, please set your height on Settings.
Go to FitPort Settings > Data Settings > Weight

2. To see your BMI, please tap and hold on the graph.
