Needs access to the Health app

Cube needs access to the Health app.

How to grant permission

iOS 13

  1. Open "Settings" app
  2. Tap "Health"
  3. Tap "Data Access & Devices"
  4. Tap "Cube"
  5. Turn All ON

iOS 12

  1. Open "Health" app
  2. Tap "Sources"
  3. Tap "Cube"
  4. Turn All ON
