How to Change the Language

Ermine only supports English and Japanese for the app itself, but it does support many languages for the widget (month name, day of the week, date), so you can use it in the language of your choice!

How to Change the Calendar Language for the Widget

  1. Open Ermine app.
  2. Tap the gear icon in the upper left corner of the screen to go to the settings.
  3. Tap "Widget Appearance".
  4. Tap "Language" in the "Other" section to select your preferred language

How to Change the Language for the App

  1. Open the "Settings" app.
  2. Tap "Ermine" from the list of apps.
  3. Tap Language to select your preferred language.
