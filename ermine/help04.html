How to Use iCloud, Google, Exchange, or Yahoo calendars

Add an account with the calendar service you want to use in Settings app, then it will be available in Ermine.

  1. Open Settings app on your device.
  2. Go to Calendar > Accounts, and tap Add Account.
  3. Tap a service (iCloud or Microsoft Exchange, for example) you want to use, then sign in to your account.
  4. Open Ermine app
  5. Tap ⚙️ icon on the top left corner, then tap Event Calendars (or Holiday Calendars).
  6. Select the calendar
