How to Use iCloud, Google, Exchange, or Yahoo calendars

Add the account with the calendar service you want to use in Settings app, then it will be available in Ermine.

These calendar services allow you to synchronize your calendar data between devices.

How to Set Up

  1. Open Settings app on your device.
  2. Go to Calendar > Accounts, and tap Add Account.
  3. Tap a service (iCloud or Microsoft Exchange, for example) you want to use, then sign in to your account.
  4. Once you've signed in, open Apple's Calendar app and tap Calendar at the bottom of the screen to ensure the calendar you want to use appears on the list. (If your calendar doesn't appear there, you haven't set up your account properly. Open the Settings app again and go to Calendars > Accounts to check your account.)
  5. Open Ermine app
  6. Tap gear icon ⚙️ on the top left corner, then tap Event Calendars (or Holiday Calendars).
  7. Select calendars.

Frequency of calendar service data synchronization

The frequency of updates will vary depending on the calendar service, network connection and device settings.

How to check the device settings:

  1. Open Settings app on your device.
  2. Go to Calendar > Accounts > Fetch New Data, and check the settings.
