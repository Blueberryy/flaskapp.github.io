Widgets are Not Updating

Widgets may not be updated immediately, for example, right after placing the widget on your home screen.

Things like this may happen:

  • Event dots do not appear.
  • Holidays are not colored in the widget, even though you registered a holiday calendar.

To fix it:
Wait for a while, or open the Ermine app and add a new event, change the start day of the week, or change widget color, etc. to wake up the widgets.

If it doesn't work, restarting your phone seems to be the best way to resolve it.
