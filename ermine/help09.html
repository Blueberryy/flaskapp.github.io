Past data has been lost.

Ermine reads data from the iOS calendar. If you can't see past events anymore, it's possible that your iOS calendar is set to not sync past data.

Please follow the steps below to check your settings.

  1. Open Settings app
  2. Tap Calendar
  3. Tap Sync
  4. Select All Events
  5. Opne Apple's Calendar app, and check if the past data is displayed.
